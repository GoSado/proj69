Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1848

Anne's dates from 1847 and the church was built in 1848. Father George Green was appointed Rector from St. Chad's, and for three years was obliged to cope with; a large population of poor and often sickly people, weakened by famine and plague. He remained until 1855 when Father Allen was appointed.



School Library

Was built in 1978

This is a library where people go to read quietly and play shess and sometimes guess in 10.



School Auditorium

Was built in 1978

This is our auditorium where our headteacher come every Friday to bring out news and give certificates.
